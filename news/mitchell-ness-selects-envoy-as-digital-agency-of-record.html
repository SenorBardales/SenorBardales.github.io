Mitchell & Ness selects Envoy as digital brand and ecommerce agency partner.

Apr 24, 2017

Envoy is proud to announce a new partnership with Mitchell & Ness. Envoy will start by reimagining the website experience with an emphasis on evolving the brand and architecting a new commerce platform to boost sales performance. Future plans are to continue to iterate the site with constant content, new personalization features, support functionality and implementing new technology solutions across the entire digital ecosystem. Recently purchased from Adidas by Juggernaut Capital Partners, the nostalgic sports apparel brand is investing in digitally transforming their business to take advantage of the growing trend towards the convergence of sports and style. Through the platform, Envoy will help Mitchell & Ness activate their close partnerships with the NBA, MLB, NFL and MLS.

About Mitchell & Ness
Mitchell & Ness's roots go back more than a century, when it started as a golf and tennis apparel maker in 1904. By the 1930s, it was making on-field uniforms for major Philadelphia sports teams such as the Eagles football team and the Phillies baseball team. In the 1980s, the brand created the Mitchell & Ness Nostalgia Co. to design and sell replicas of historic sports jerseys. Mitchell & Ness holds licenses with sports leagues such as the National Football League, Major League Baseball, the National Basketball Association and Major League Soccer. Mitchell & Ness is owned by U.S. private-equity firm Juggernaut Capital Partners.
