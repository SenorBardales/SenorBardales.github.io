About

Holder of a bachelor's degree in Multimedia at Cifacom, I worked during almost 6 years for Lonsdale to build simple & smart digital experiences on web, mobile, televisions or in-store for various clients (from CAC 40 companies to startups).

I joined Sounds as Lead Designer for 2 years. Our mission was to connect the world with music by reinventing how people experience music with friends on mobile.

I worked as Lead Designer at Kard to empowers young people to take control over their money by offering them a bank that is cool, social and free.

I also worked at Powder, to build the Camera of the Metaverse (1.5m downloads in 10 months and raised $14m Series A).

I'm currently working as Head of Design at Free.
