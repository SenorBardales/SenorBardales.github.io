What’s Versus and why we built it?

Versus is inspired by the "Would you rather" game. WYR is a conversation or party game that poses a dilemma in the form of a question beginning with "would you rather". The dilemma can be between two supposedly good options, two attractive choices, or it can be between two supposedly bad options. The players, sometimes including the questioner, then must choose their answers. Answering "neither" or "both" is against the rules.

The goal was to re-create this fun experience in an iOS application. There's already a lot of apps like that on the store but many of them looks outdated and designed for a solo use. We wanted social features at the core of the experience and with the hype on Snapkit it was a perfect opportunity.

We want to create that social experience by encouraging users to invite their friends to answer questions. This leads the players to debate their rationales and compare their answers.
