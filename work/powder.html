About the project

Powder aspires to become the camera of the Metaverse. People increasingly live online, in games, and it hopes to allow them to capture their highlights here for perpetuity. To enable this, Powder is building the video infrastructure of social gaming, sourcing content from all gaming platforms: the ultimate companion app from which anyone can launch games with friends and that records highlights automatically.
